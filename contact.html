Hello! I'm Yue, a passionate creator and proactive designer.

I mainly focus on experience design, combined with the most interesting and creative technology. I love to ask questions about our daily life, and try to answer them in a positive and playful way. Currently, I'm an MFA Design and Technology student at Parsons School of Design in NYC. (expected grad in May, 2020)

I always put users at the center of my design, tring to fulfill their needs, as well as finding out the problems in a larger context. I like how physical computing can bring magical ideas to life. I enjoy playing and making games, which I think is one of the best forms of interaction.
