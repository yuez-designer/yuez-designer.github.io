融音 PRESS, MIX & PLAY

JAN. 2019 -- FEB. 2019 / GROUP PROJECT

Press, Mix & Play (“融音” in Chinese) is a interactive music installation in Chinatown, NYC. The goal of this project is to give Chinese elder people a opportunity to communicate with other age groups, and get a sense of belonging on this foreign land.

I’m Leader, User Researcher, UX Designer in the group.
