Doraemon Bamboo Copter Game Prototype

Nov. 2019 / Group Project

Demo Video

In this fun experience, the player will play as Doraemon. The goal of this game is to stop Nobita Nobi before he arrives Shizuka Minamotor’s house and does stupid things. The MPU 6050 sensor is used in this game, the player can control the Bamboo Copter’s direction by rotating their head. There are two push button on player’s hand which is use to control the Doraemon’s thrust. The only way to stop Nobita in this game is to shot him down. The player can do a cross hand gesture in order to shot.

My roles in the group: physical computing technologist and wearable maker.
